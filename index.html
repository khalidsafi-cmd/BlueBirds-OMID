About BlueBirds OMID Organization

About BBO

The bluebird is a global symbol of happiness, hope, and renewal. Our mission is to help street-working children in Afghanistan overcome hardship and create a brighter future, much like a bluebird soaring freely in the sky. “Omid” means “hope” in Persian — a promise we carry to give children the chance to dream again.

Read more

Our Mission

No child should trade their dreams for survival. We are here to lift them up, replace struggle with opportunity, and turn despair into hope. Through education, financial support, and unwavering care, we give these children the wings to rise above hardship and soar toward a future filled with possibility.

Read more

Our Vision

We envision a future where no child is left behind — where education replaces child labor, where hope overcomes hardship, and where every child has the chance to build a life of dignity and opportunity.
